Description of our Project

Our project, “A Day In My Life” Journal, is a personal diary web application designed for individuals who want to document their daily experiences, emotions, and memories in a safe, digital space. The purpose of the platform is to make journaling more convenient and creative, allow users to track their mood patterns, and reflect on personal growth over time. Unlike traditional text-only journals, this system lets users express themselves through text, images, audio, or video entries while also tracking their mood and receiving daily motivational quotes

The Problem Our Website Will Solve

Many people struggle to keep up with traditional journaling due to time, privacy, and organisation issues. Others want to go beyond text entries and include photos, voice notes, or short videos that reflect their day. Existing apps often lack these media types or require premium subscriptions. Our website solves this problem by providing an all-in-one, free, and user-friendly journaling system that securely stores text and multimedia entries in a database while also allowing users to view motivational quotes and analyse their emotional trends.
